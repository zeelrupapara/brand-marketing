Frequently asked questions

Clear answers before the first conversation.

What does Haley Mae manage?

Haley Mae provides brand strategy, messaging, digital brand management, executive branding, reputation oversight, organic social media planning, brand consistency reviews, and photography and video coordination.

Does Haley Mae design websites?

No. Haley Mae does not provide or coordinate website design. The focus is how an organization’s brand, messaging, photography, content, and public presentation appear across its existing digital platforms.

Does Haley Mae provide SEO or paid advertising?

No. Haley Mae does not provide SEO, paid advertising, media buying, or campaign management. Its work is centered on brand presentation, reputation, consistency, and organic communication.

Can Haley Mae work with an internal marketing team?

Yes. Haley Mae can work alongside leadership and internal marketing teams to establish priorities, improve consistency, and provide focused brand direction.

Is photography or videography included?

Creative direction and coordination may be included. Photographer, videographer, studio, location, and outside production expenses are quoted separately.

Which types of organizations does Haley Mae serve?

Haley Mae works with established and growing organizations that value reputation, clarity, and a consistent public presence. Engagements are selected based on the organization’s needs and goals—not limited to a specific industry.

Where does Haley Mae work?

Haley Mae is based in Atlanta and works with select organizations nationwide.

How does an engagement begin?

The process begins with a complimentary Private Introductory Brand Review to discuss the organization’s public presence, current priorities, and areas of opportunity.
