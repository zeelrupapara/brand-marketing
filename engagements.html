Monthly Brand Stewardship

Brand Presence works with clients through monthly retainer engagements.

Each engagement is customized based on the organization’s size, number of platforms, content needs, reputation oversight, leadership involvement, and photography or video coordination requirements.

Our work is designed for organizations that want ongoing brand stewardship rather than one-time marketing support.

Creative production, including photography, videography, and outside design work, is quoted separately when needed.

To determine the right level of support, we begin with a brand review and consultation.

Typical Engagement Areas

  • Monthly brand presentation review
  • Organic social media planning and management
  • Content calendar development
  • Photography and video coordination
  • Online reputation monitoring
  • Leadership and executive presence support
  • Brand consistency recommendations
  • Monthly reporting and strategy review

Who This Is For

Our engagements are best suited for organizations that care about how they are perceived and want consistent, professional oversight of their public-facing digital presence.

We work best with clients who value long-term brand credibility, reputation, and consistency.

What We Do Not Provide

Brand Presence is focused on digital brand presentation and brand management.

We do not provide:

Website management
SEO

PPC advertising

Software development

This allows us to stay focused on what we do best: improving how your organization is seen, understood, and trusted.
